Find A Stakepool
Stakepools allow you to participate in Decred Proof-of-Stake (PoS) mining without needing to worry
about losses due computer downtime, flaky internet connectivity, or inconsistent power.
Notice: Missed tickets can be caused by a poorly connected Proof-of-work (PoW) miner and may not necessarily be the fault of the pool.
Warning: To protect de-centralization of the network, it is recommended to avoid pools that have over 5% of network votes.
