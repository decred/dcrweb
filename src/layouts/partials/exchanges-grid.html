Decred OTC Traders
Over The Counter (OTC) trading allows customers to buy or sell Decred directly from a trader. The customer and the trader agree on a fixed price for a large quantity of Decred and conduct the transaction without going through an exchange. The minimum transaction size for OTC trading is typically $100,000 or higher.
